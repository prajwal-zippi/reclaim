Refund & cancellation policy.

How refunds work for the campaign logistics fee (minimum ₹500).

The logistics fee

The logistics fee (minimum ₹500) paid with a campaign application covers the collection vehicle, crew and materials for your scheduled campaign day. It is a service fee, not a donation.

If you cancel

Cancel at least 48 hours before your scheduled campaign date and we will refund the fee in full. Cancellations within 48 hours of the scheduled date are not refundable, because the vehicle and crew are already committed.

If we cancel or cannot serve you

If we cannot schedule your campaign (for example, your location is outside our service area) or we cancel for any reason, you receive a full refund automatically.

How refunds are paid

Refunds are sent by UPI or bank transfer to the account the payment came from, normally within 5–7 business days of your request being approved.

How to request one

Email reclaimera@gmail.com or call +91 81520 20145 with your UPI transaction reference (UTR) and the application reference shown on your success screen.
