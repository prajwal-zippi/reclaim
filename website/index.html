How we work

From doorstep to classroom, nothing is wasted.

Four verbs power everything we do. Together they form a closed loop that turns clutter into learning.

01

We Collect

Hyper-local pickups, corporate collection drives, and campus Revive Stations across Bengaluru.

02

We Revive

Wearable items are sanitised and donated to BPL families and transit communities in need.

03

We Recycle

Unusable fabrics are diverted to industrial shredders, so nothing we collect goes to landfill.

04

We Rise

Heavy fabrics are upcycled into premium products; proceeds fund LEACT's education programs.
