Core processes

Every item gets a second act.

Sorting & Segregation

Every item that arrives at Kogilu is evaluated and triaged as wearable, upcyclable or recyclable.

Upcycling

Durable, heavy fabrics become premium products in the hands of EmpowHer artisans.

Downcycling

Heavily soiled items are processed into industrial inputs, so nothing goes to landfill.

The journey

From your doorstep to impact, in four steps.

Hyper-local collection

Drop-offs, neighbourhood drives, and campus Revive Stations across Bengaluru.

Sorting & triage

Items are evaluated at the Kogilu hub and routed by condition and material.

Revive / Recycle / Rise

Donated to communities, diverted to shredders, or upcycled into products.

Sales & impact routing

Proceeds flow to LEACT to fund teachers, notebooks and school infrastructure.
