Environmental education, hands-on.

Move from textbook theory to real-world action with a year-long curriculum aligned to NEP 2020 and NCF 2023.

Partner Your School

Program components

A curriculum that gets its hands dirty.

Interactive workshops

Project-based learning that turns waste literacy into a school-year adventure.

Student-led Zero Waste Audits

Students audit their own campus and run on-site Revive Stations.

Teacher training

Educators get full training and materials, aligned to NEP 2020 & NCF 2023.

Why it works

Behaviour change starts in the classroom.

Our one-year program embeds circular thinking into daily school life. Students don't just learn about waste. They weigh it, sort it and divert it, then teach their families to do the same.

  • 11 government schools already partnered across Bengaluru.
  • 1,600 students targeted for environmental education by 2030.
  • Board games, toys and notebooks supplied to classrooms, funded by the upcycle shop.

What your school receives

  • A 1-year, ready-to-run environmental curriculum
  • On-campus Revive Station set-up and signage
  • Teacher training and ongoing support
  • Student zero-waste audit toolkits
  • End-of-year impact certificate for the school
